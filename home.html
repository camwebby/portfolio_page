Programming & Development


In-Cahoots

In Cahoots is a Django web application which provides a solution to my University's following problems:
• Societies have a capability of helping other society’s development with their respective skillset.
• Societies often struggle to find meaningful projects and activities to engage their members with.
A platform that would facilitate societies helping one another would, not only accelerate development of societies, but also provide societies with meaningful projects. “In Cahoots” is just that – an online platform that could become a sustained ecosystem of societies helping one another.
